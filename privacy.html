Privacy Policy

This application is designed to function as a local data organization and documentation tool.

All information entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s device. The app does not transmit, collect, store, or access user data externally. No accounts are created, and no data is synced or shared automatically.

Users may choose to export their own data in PDF format for personal record-keeping or reporting purposes. Exported files are generated locally on the device and are shared only at the user’s discretion using the device’s standard sharing options.

The app does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking technologies. The developer does not have access to any data entered into the app.
